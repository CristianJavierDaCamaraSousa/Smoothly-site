# Smoothly Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** June 2026

Smoothly is designed to run locally on your PC. We do not operate user accounts or analytics servers for the app.

## What the app does on your computer

| Data | Purpose | Leaves your PC? |
|------|---------|-----------------|
| Settings (`%AppData%\Smoothly\settings.json`) | Your preferences and license state | No |
| Trial start date (registry `HKCU\Software\Smoothly`) | Enforce 14-day trial | No |
| Log files (`%AppData%\Smoothly\logs`) | Troubleshooting | No |
| Global mouse hook | Smooth scrolling | No |

## Network access

Smoothly may connect to the internet only when:

- **Check for updates** is enabled (reads `version.json` from the public website)
- You click **Buy**, **Support**, **EULA**, or **download update** (opens your browser)

No personal data is sent to us by the app itself.

## License keys

License keys are validated **offline** using cryptography embedded in the app. We do not receive your key unless you contact support and choose to share it.

## Payment

Purchases are processed by our payment provider (e.g. Lemon Squeezy or Gumroad). Their privacy policy applies to checkout. We receive your email and order information from them to fulfill your license.
